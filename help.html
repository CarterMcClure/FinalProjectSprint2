Welcome to Pixel Playground! An interface of 3 amazing games, each of which providing hours of fun

Below are in-depth controls for all 3 of the games!

     

Tetris

Tetris is a game where the objective is to rotate the geometric shapes to form complete lines at the bottom of the game. All the controls are consolodated to the keypad. You can move left or right. Pressing up rotates the shape and pressing down makes the blocks fall faster.

Snake

In Snake, the objective is to get the snake as long as possible. Move around with the keypad and try to collect the bits. As your snake gets longer, try to not move in a way which makes the snake hit itself, because this will end your game.

Runner

Runner is simple. Use the spacebar to jump and avoid obsticles to increase your distance and score!
